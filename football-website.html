Emiliano Martinez

It is Martinez who has played the most decisive role in Argentina's campaign so far, as his three penalty saves against Colombia handed Argentina a ticket to Rio de Janeiro for the final.

Ederson

Ederson Santana de Moraes, known as Ederson, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Premier League club Manchester City and the Brazil national team.

Angel Di Maria

The victory was sealed courtesy a goal by Angel Di Maria in the 22nd minute of the match. The PSG forward beat the off side trap to receive a long floater from the mid-field then chipped the ball into the net to perfection.

Thiago Silva

The first of this weekend’s major international finals took place in Rio de Janeiro last night, with Thiago Silva’s Brazil side defeated by Argentina 1-0 in the Copa America final.

Marquinhos

Marquinhos, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a defender for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain, and the Brazil national team. Mainly a central defender also can play on defensive midfielder.

Casemiro

Lucas Diaz

Colombian Luis Diaz was named Revelation of the Copa America. The Porto forward scored two goals in the third-place playoff win over Peru and was the tournament’s joint top goalscorer along with Lionel Messi.

Rodrigo de Paul

Rodrigo de Paul's brilliant pass to Angel di Maria was simply superb and it helped Argentina break the deadlock in the Copa America final against Brazil.

Cristian Romero
